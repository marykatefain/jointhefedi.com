Social Media By and For The People

The Fediverse puts the power of social media in the hands of the people by decentralizing communication platforms. Rather than one company that controls it all, the Fedverse consists of thousands of compeltely independent "servers" each able to speak to the others.

Decentralized Network

Decentralized networks are resilient to censorship, because if one platform "cancels" you, you can simply pick up and move to another server.

Free and Open Source Software

All Fediverse servers are completely free and open source software, which means that anyone can build their own community.

Choose a Community

There are thousands of "servers" on the Fediverse, and each is totally unique with their own rules, themes, and even features. Choose a community that is right for you, and move at any time.

Speak Your Mind

Whether you choose a "free speech" server or a more moderated one, you can rest assured that corporations can't control and censor you on the Fediverse.
